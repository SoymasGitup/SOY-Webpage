Quality

Alimentos Texturizados S.A. implements various standards, procedures and protocols to obtain high-quality, safe food products.

Our process operates under Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) and Standardized Sanitation Operating Procedures (SSOP). We are also working on implementing a Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Points (HACCP) system, to provide our clients with confidence and safety in our SOY+ products.

We hold KOSHER and HALAL certifications, and our work system is oriented towards the continuous training of our staff in HACCP.

Our facility is approved and registered by SENASA (Argentina's National Food Safety and Quality Service). We hold a National Establishment Registry (RNE) and a National Food Product Registry (RNPA), complying with all applicable regulations.
